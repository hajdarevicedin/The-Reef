Beach Weddings

Who doesn’t want their wedding day to be perfect? Many of us start planning our big day when we’re only little as we dream of finding that special somebody and celebrating our love with all our friends and family.

Dining options are flexible and can be either sit-down or buffet. The Reef is also the perfect setting for rehearsal dinners, bridal showers, or buffet lunches. Our banquet coordinator offers professional services to assist you in all your needs.

Our Offers

Whatever your style and tastes are, we have the option that’s right for you. Whether you’re after a wedding that’s classic and screams elegance, or one that’ll wow your guests and elevate your wedding to iconic status, we’ve got you covered.

Perhaps you’d feel more at home with a modern feel to your special day instead? Or if you’re particularly adventurous, then discover one of our more industrial options to make your wedding even more unique.
